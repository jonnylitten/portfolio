Topology Morpher

Interactive 3D parametric surface visualizer using Three.js. Real-time morphing between mathematical surfaces (torus, sphere, Klein bottle) with deformation parameters. Maps Gaussian curvature to audio frequencies for sonification of geometric properties. Demonstrates understanding of differential geometry and audio-visual synthesis.

Harmonic Constellation

Gravitational music generator combining sacred geometry, n-body physics simulation, and real-time audio synthesis. Users spawn orbital bodies along parametric curves (Golden Spiral, Fibonacci, Lissajous, Rose curves) that generate harmonic interactions through gravitational forces. Implements proper scale theory with modal selections and MIDI output support.

Gravity Well Orchestra

N-body gravity simulator with musical mapping. Launch orbital bodies into gravitational fields and create harmonic resonances when orbital periods align with musical intervals (octaves, fifths, fourths). Implements proper gravitational physics with numerical stability via substeps, orbital period detection through angle crossing, and Web Audio synthesis of detected harmonies.

Quantum Sequencer

Probabilistic music sequencer using wave function collapse mechanics. Each grid cell exists in superposition until observed, with probability fields determining note generation. Click to collapse wavefunctions manually or let the playhead observe columns sequentially. Implements uncertainty-based composition with adjustable probability density and quantum entanglement between neighboring cells.
